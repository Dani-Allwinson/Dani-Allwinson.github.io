Palipalaiyam Chicken

Description

Pallipalayam Chicken is famous among the dhabas and restaurants in and around erode. The spiciness and that special authentic taste of the dish makes it famous. Because this will not contain too much masala in it and it is mouth watering.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Sesame oil
  • Shallots (Small Onion)
  • Dry red chillies
  • Sliced Coconut
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Coriander Powder
  • Tumeric Powder
  • Salt
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Curry Leaves

Steps

  1. Frist grind the shallots along with garlic in mixer grinder and keep it aside.
  2. Heat the pan with oil on medium flame , add dry red chillies and allow it to sizzle for a few seconds , next add the curry leaves and allow it to crakle. Add onion-garlic mixture and saulte till the raw smellgoes away.
  3. It will take about 10-12 minute, Once the onion mixture appears cooked add all the spice powders - red chili powder and tumeric podwer . Continue to cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Add Sliced Chicken and sprikle some salt and mix it well.Keep sauting till the chicken is rosted well.Cook for about 20 minutes adn make sure the chicken s cooked.
  5. Switch off teh heat , garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with white rice.
