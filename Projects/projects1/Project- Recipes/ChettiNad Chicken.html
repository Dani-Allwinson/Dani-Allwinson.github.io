ChettiNad Chicken

Discription

Chicken Chettinad or Chettinad chicken is a classic Indian recipe, from the cuisine of Chettinad, Tamil Nadu.
This is usually the spicy chicken preparation of any chicken recipe , an authentic and most tastiest chicken recipe ever.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • Coriander Seeds
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Curry Leaves
  • Poopy Seeds
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Vinegar
  • Dry Red Chilli
  • Coconut
  • Green Chilli
  • Coriander Leaves
  • Lemon Juice
  • Tumeric Powder
  • Whole Spices
  • Tomato
  • Red Chilli Powder

Steps

  1. Mix the chicken with 1 teaspoon salt and vinegar and keep aside for 1/2 and hour.
  2. In a pan , heat 2 teaspoon of oil when the oil is hot . Add two springs of curry leaves , coriander seeds , cumin seeds ,
    fennel seeds , poppy seeds, cloves ,cinnamon,cardamom , black peppercorns , and 8-10 dry chilies
  3. Fry till slightly browned and a nice aroma starts coming. Add Â½ cup of chopped onion and grated coconut and fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add giner-garlic paste and fry for another 3-4 minutes. Cool and grind the mixture in a grinder , add water to grind the ingredients
  5. At the end , the grinded ingredients should by smooth paste.
  6. Add 3 tablespoon oil in a pan. Add 2-3 springs of curry leaves and remaining onion.
  7. Fry the onion till it comes to golden brown and add green chilli , tumeric powder , red chili powder and salt.
  8. Add tomato and the roasted and ground mixture . Now add a cup of water and cook for a minute and add chicken and another cup of water and cover and cook till chickenn is done.
  9. Add lime juice and fresh coriander and cook for a minute.
  10. Serve hot with paratha or Naan.
