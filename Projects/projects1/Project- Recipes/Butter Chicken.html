Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken is also known as Chicken Makhani is a classic Indian Dish . It look like buttery and spicy , armotic and creamy tomato gravy.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Olive oil
  • Coriander
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Almonds
  • Cashews
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Sugar
  • Tomato
  • Dried Coriander
  • Garam Masala
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Heavy Cream
  • Kasuri Methi

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken - Take teh Chicken and teh rest of the marinade ingredients together in a large bowl. Use hands to massage the chicken to spread marinade into the chicken.
  2. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for a least 1 hour, or up to overnight.
  3. Soak the Cashews adn Almonds in the water for at least an hour or overnight before preparing the rest of the recipe.
  4. Heat the pan over the medium-high heat, Pour in one teaspoon of oil in the pan.
  5. Remove the chicken fromt the marinade and grill them on the pan for 3 or 5 minute on each side , until the chicken is cooked through clear juice runs out of it when you cut into it.
  6. Once cooked , transfer the chicken into a separate dish. Cover and set aside for later.
  7. To make a Sauce - Melt 1 1/2 table sppons of butter tn a medium sized pan on medium low heat.
  8. As soon as the butter melts , add the giner garlic paste follwed by sugar. Salute for 1 to 2 minutes.
  9. Stir int he tomato and cook for 10 minute on medium high heat , until all the extra liquid is cooked off. The sauce should be a loose, paste-like consistency.
  10. Add coriander,Garam masala, Red Chilli powder and salt . Stir and cook on medium heat for 2-3 minute and later turn off the heat.
  11. Now, transfer the paste to a blender or the bowl of a food processor. Add the soaked Cashews adn Almonds blend into a smooth paste.
  12. Return the paste to the cooking pan over medium heat. Add the water to the paste and stir to make a smooth sauce.
  13. As the sauce begins to boil , add the grilled chicken and heavy cream . Stir well and Later cover teh pan and simmer for 5 minutes.
  14. Turn off the heat and unciver the pan, stir in teh remaining 1/2 tablespoon butter and the kasuri methi. Cover again, and let the dish rest for 8-10 minutes so the flavor of kasuri methi can marry into the butter chicken.
  15. Atlast, serve the butter chicken with Steamed Rice,Roti,or Naan .
